# Wiki: Real Estate Payment Terms (Schedule-E)
This document outlines the structured payment milestones and financial obligations for the acquisition and development of a plot as per **Schedule-E**.
## 1. Overview of Payment Components
To ensure clarity across all milestones, the following items are collectively referred to as the **Primary Cost Basket**:
 * Plot Basic Cost
 * Development Charges
 * Preferential Location Charges (PLC)
 * Transformer and Electric Charges
 * Club Membership
## 2. Milestone Breakdown
Payments are triggered by specific administrative actions or physical construction progress on-site.
| Milestone | Payment Percentage / Amount | Additional Fees & Deductions | Taxes |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Expression of Interest (EOI)** | ₹50,000 | N/A | + GST |
| **Booking Letter** | 10% of Primary Cost Basket | Less EOI (₹50,000) already paid | + GST |
| **On Agreement** | 10% of Primary Cost Basket | 50% of Legal Charges | + GST |
| **Commencement of Approach Road** | 20% of Primary Cost Basket | Before laying of Paver Blocks | + GST |
| **Commencement of Plot Leveling** | 20% of Primary Cost Basket | N/A | + GST |
| **Commencement of Plot Fencing** | 20% of Primary Cost Basket | N/A | + GST |
| **Handover / Possession** | 20% of Primary Cost Basket | 50% of Legal Charges + 100% Association Formation + 100% Sinking Fund | + GST |
## 3. Detailed Stage Descriptions
### Stage 1: Initial Reservation
 * **EOI:** A flat fee of **₹50,000** is required to express interest.
 * **Booking:** Formalizes the intent to purchase. The 10% calculation includes all primary costs, with the initial EOI amount deducted from the total due at this stage.
### Stage 2: Documentation
 * **Agreement Stage:** Triggered upon the execution of the formal purchase agreement. This stage requires the first half (**50%**) of the **Legal Charges**.
### Stage 3: Infrastructure Development
Payments are linked to the physical progress of the project infrastructure:
 1. **Approach Road:** Triggered when work starts on the road leading to the plot, specifically *before* paver blocks are installed.
 2. **Plot Leveling:** Triggered when the physical leveling of the specific plot begins.
 3. **Plot Fencing:** Triggered when the perimeter fencing work for the plot commences.
### Stage 4: Final Possession
The final stage completes the financial transfer and includes the remaining administrative and maintenance funds:
 * **Legal Balance:** The remaining **50% of Legal Charges**.
 * **Association Formation:** **100%** of the charges required to form the resident/owner association.
 * **Sinking Fund:** **100%** contribution to the project’s long-term maintenance fund.
> **Note on Taxation:** Every installment and additional charge listed above is subject to the prevailing **Goods and Services Tax (GST)** rates as per statutory requirements.
