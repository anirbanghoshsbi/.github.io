Elder Impulse System
Green Impulse : Up 13 EMA wrt yesterday and MACD Hisogram today > MACD histogram yesterday.
Red Impulse: Down 13 EMA wrt yesterday and MACD Histogram Today < MACD Histogram yesterday
Blue Impulse: otherwise.

Entry Long Condition : Every Indian NSE Market Open Day at 3.15 pm if Impulse is Green while previous day impulse is non green plus Close today > 20 Day EMA of Nifty while yesterday Close < 20 EMA.

Entry Short Condition : Every Indian NSE Market Open Day at 3.15 pm if Impulse is Red while previous day impulse is non Red plus Close today < 20 Day EMA of Nifty while yesterday Close > 20 EMA.

Valuation and Liquidity :
PE Percentile [Valuation Metric] :
If PE percentile <=20% undervalued (1)
If PE percentile beteween 20% and 40% fairvalued (2)
If PE percentile beteween 60% and 80% High Valuation (4)
If PE percentile beteween >=80%  Overvaluation Valuation (5)
If PE percentile between 40% and 60% Market Neutral (2).

Div Yield Percentile [Valuation Metric] :
If Div Yield percentile >=80% undervalued (1)
If Div Yield percentile beteween 60% and 80% fairvalued (2)
If Div Yield percentile beteween 20% and 30% High Valuation (4)
If Div Yield percentile beteween <=20%  Overvaluation Valuation (5)
If Div Yield percentile between 40% and 60% Market Neutral (2).
